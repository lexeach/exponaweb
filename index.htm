    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is EXPONA?
    EXPONA is a global, new generation crowdfunding platform and the first ever smart contract of its kind in the blockchain of Binance Smart Chain It is a software algorithm performing the function of a distribution of affiliate commissions between community members.

    How are commissions paid?
    All commissions are paid in BNB directly and instantly to the members’ personal BNB wallets. The smart contract never receives payments; therefore, you will never have to wait on a commission payment as it’s paid directly to you.
    What is the risk of joining EXPONA?
    EXPONA is risk-free! There is no company, no admin, it is a self-operating system located on the blockchain that cannot be changed or destroyed. The code of the smart contract is in the public domain. All payments go directly into your personal wallet, without any hidden fees and without third-party resources. You can feel safe knowing that EXPONA will not go away.
    What personal information is needed to open an account?
    No personal information is required to open an account. The only thing required to create your account is a BNB wallet of your choice.
    How long can EXPONA last?
    EXPONA is a Smart Contract on the Binance Smart Chain which is fully decentralized and connected to the internet. EXPONA will be in business for as long as the Binance Smart Chain and internet exists.
    Do I ever lose the people I refer to EXPONA?
    In EXPONA, your entire downline including direct , indirect, overflow or spill over follows you at all times in every step of the matrix.You will pass up a commission to your upline if you are not active on a position that generates an earning.However, once you become active on that level, you will get paid for any further sales that take place from that point onwards.
    Is it compulsory to recruit to earn from the system?
    No. EXPONA platform is arranged in such a way that you willl receive Auto-fill matrix income passively However, to make a lot of money, doing nothing will not work. You must try and help people build their teams, and once they are auto pilot , you will earn passively. The more active you are initially will determine how quickly you will reach this auto- earning mode.
    Can my account ever be closed?
    No, once your account is created it can never be removed or destroyed.
    Is there a limit to the amount of BNB you can earn?
    You can genuinely earn UNLIMITED BNB in this business. The more people you refer and the more people you assist with growing their business, the higher your income potential becomes.
    how is the registration fee distrubuted in EXPONA?
    Smart contract distributes the registration fee in three parts:
    • Sponsor income 20% part goes to sponsor, sponsor is the person who explain EXPONA to new user
    • Level income 40% part of registration fee distributed in sponsor level tree, up to ten level divided equally (4%) on each level
    • Global Auto Pool Matrix 40% part of registration fee distributed in Global Auto Pool Matrix, up to five level divided equally (8%) on each level
    Who owns EXPONA?
    EXPONA platform is created with the public’s interest in mind and does not have managers or admins. This means that the platform is fully decentralized (i.e. it has no company, no leaders, or admins) and cannot be changed or altered at any time.
    What is a Smart Contract?
    A smart contract is nothing but a predetermined algorithm inside the blockchain which automates the execution of transactions.
    What is Decentralization?
    A system in which there are no admins, no servers, and no monitoring needed to manage the project. The creators of the platform are the same project participants like you.
    I don't have Referral link, can I register with EXPONA?
    Yes,You will be Register under Systems Referral link using ID number 1 as SponsorID.
    Why is smart contract unverified?
    It has been seen that when an innovative smart contract launched, it has been copied in large numbers in the market. EXPONA smart contract kept unverified so that no one can copy and make duplicate smart contract.
    How can users know the registration fee?
    As we know registration fee for EXPONA smart contract is dynamic, users can see running registration fee by clicking "REGISTRATION FESS" button given at read section of contract dashboard.

Other smart contract facing the same problem. If price of cryptocurrency used by smart contract go much high or much low it can be irrelevant over time.Stable coin solve this problem,but temporarily,If promoter of stable coin decide to switch the blockchain then it will a more bigger problem to the users. If the price of BNB goes up or down, registration fee will be always equivalent to $100(approximately),means user need to purchase BNB equivalent to $100 to register. Here with EXPONA always, no matter what is the price of BNB in future and You can play a long time inning with EXPONA.
